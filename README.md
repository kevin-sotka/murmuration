# Murmuration

Draw the sky. A flock of three hundred starlings follows your finger through rings of dusk light while a falcon hunts the heart of the swarm.

**Play it:** https://kevin-sotka.github.io/murmuration/

## How to play
Touch or click and drag anywhere: the flock flows toward your pointer. Thread the glowing rings to score and build a combo. When the red glint flashes at the screen edge, pull the flock away before the falcon strikes. The run ends when fewer than 60 starlings remain.

## Tech
One self-contained `index.html`. Canvas 2D boids with a spatial hash grid, synthesized WebAudio sound, zero dependencies, zero asset downloads.

Part of [The Aviary](https://kevin-sotka.github.io/the-aviary/), three bird games competing for your vote. Built by the SodClaw Game Foundry.
